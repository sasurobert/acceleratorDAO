### Liquidity-Volume-Fee Approximations
DeFi relationships follow clear formulas, backed by 2025 data. Assume good-faith averages; actuals vary by market conditions.

- **DEX Formula**: Fees = Volume × Fee Rate (0.2-0.3%). Volume ≈ TVL × Velocity (3-7x daily, or ~1,000-2,500x yearly). Example: $X=1M TVL → $Y=3B yearly volume (3x daily) → $Z=6-9M fees (0.2-0.3%).
- **Lending Formula**: Fees = Borrowed × Interest Rate (6-7% APR avg.). Borrowed ≈ TVL × Utilization (60-80%). Example: $X=1M TVL → $Y=700K borrowed (70%) → $Z=42-49K yearly fees (6-7%).
- **Liquid Staking Formula**: Fees = Staked × Yield (3-5%) × Commission (10%). Example: $X=1M staked → $Y=40K rewards (4%) → $Z=4K protocol fees (10%).
- **Perpetuals (Industry Standard)**: Fees = Volume × Fee Rate (6 bps). Volume ≈ OI × Turnover (10x daily, or ~3,650x yearly). Example: $X=1M OI → $Y=36.5B yearly volume → $Z=2.2M fees (0.06%).
